Department of Information Science & Engineering


The department of Information Science was established in the year 1999, accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) a premier board promoting quality technical education in India, and Afflicated TO VTU Belgaum and recognised by AICTE NEW DELHI.


The ABC’s of Information Science program is rated as one of the best under graduate program offered in Bangalore with an intake of 60 seats annually; it is one of the core departments in ABC engineering college headed by AG NATRAJ. The faculty members are well qualified and have more than 20 years of experience in teaching field.


The department has smart class rooms and well equipped laboratories. The department has grabbed 04 gold medals and 03 ranks from VTU till date. The academic activities of the Department lays emphasis on deep understanding of fundamental concepts, development of creative ability to handle the challenges of Information Science Engineering, and the analytical ability to solve engineering problems which are interdisciplinary in nature.

Faculty members of the department have produced more than 200 research articles in the reputed Journals and Conference proceedings. The published papers have high citation index.


The ABC’S Information science engineering is considered as one among the best information science engineering course offered in bangalore The AMC’S electronic and communication engineering involves Analyze, design and provide engineering solutions in the areas with core competence in mathematics, scientific, and basic, engineering fundamentals to develop software applications and skills students in core areas of system engineering, information processing and application development.
